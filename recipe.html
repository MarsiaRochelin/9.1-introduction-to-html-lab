Irish Coffee

Description

Make your next morning have an extra umph to it or make your next coffee date be more interesting with my take on a Irish coffee (Don't say I never gave you nothing).

Ingredients

  • A sppon of Instant Coffee
  • A square or 2 of Hersheys milk Chocolate
  • A spoon of Sugar(optional)
  • A cup of boiling hot Water
  • A shot or 2 or 3 of Alcohol (of your choice)
  • Pour Cream (to your liking)

Directions

  1. Get a cup and add sugar (I use brown sugar) to your liking.
  2. In addition to the sugar it is optional add a square or two of Hershey's milk chocolate (to cut your sugar intake you can substitute the sugar with the chocolate or vise versa)
  3. Add a spoon of your favorite instant blend of coffee(I use coffee Busetlo)
  4. Pour boiling hot water into your cup)
  5. Then, add a shot or 2 or 3 of the liqour of your choice (mine is Hennessy in your coffee then lastly add cream to your liking.
  6. Lastly, add cream to your liking, stir, and sip.
